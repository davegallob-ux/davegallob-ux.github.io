Schnopsen Counter - Support

Welcome to the support page for Schnopsen Counter, your reliable companion for tracking Bummerl and points in the classic Austrian card game.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How do I start a new game?
Simply tap "Spiel starten" on the landing page, enter the player names, and choose who starts as the dealer.

2. What are "Bummerl"?
In Schnopsen, Bummerl are penalty points given to the loser of a round. Our app automatically calculates these based on the final score (e.g., Schneider or Schuster rules).

3. How do I remove ads?
Go to Settings (Einstellungen) and select "Werbung entfernen." This is a one-time in-app purchase that supports the development of the app.

4. The screen turns off while playing. What can I do?
By default, the app keeps the screen active. You can toggle this in the Settings menu under "Bildschirm anlassen."
